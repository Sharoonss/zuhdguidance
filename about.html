About Us

Vision & History

Zuhd aims to inspire hearts and minds through authentic Islamic content, rooted in the teachings of the Quran and sunnah. Founded in 2019, we have grown into a leading platform dedicated to fostering spiritual and educational growth for Muslims around the world.

Founders

Dr. Sami Al-Hashmi, an esteemed Islamic scholar, founded Zuhd with a vision to provide high-quality Islamic lectures and resources.
